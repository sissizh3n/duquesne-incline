Directions By Map


Get Here by Bus

  • Take the 45 Southbound from Downtown Station toward Lakeside.
  • Transfer at Main & 12th to the Route 89 Crosstown.
  • Ride for 6 stops and get off at Riverpark Boulevard.
  • Walk east along Riverpark for two blocks until you reach Maple Avenue.
  • Turn right onto Maple; the entrance is halfway down the block on your left.

Get Here by Train

  • Board the Red Line heading southbound from Central Station.
  • Stay on for 3 stops and exit at East Junction.
  • Transfer to the Blue Line toward Hillcrest.
  • Get off at Northwood Station (second stop).
  • Exit the station and take the pedestrian bridge to Oak Street.
  • Walk 5 minutes down Oak until you reach Elm Drive.
  • The building will be directly ahead, across from the gas station.

Get Here by Car

  • From I-90 East, take Exit 42 for Broadview Avenue.
  • Turn left at the end of the ramp and continue for 1.5 miles.
  • At the second roundabout, take the third exit onto Pinehurst Road.
  • Merge right after the traffic light to stay on Pinehurst.
  • Turn right onto Cedar Lane just past the community center.
  • The destination is on the left, with free parking in the lot behind the building.
